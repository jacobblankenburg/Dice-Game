Rules of the Game

  • A roll of 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 11 or 12 results in that amount of points.
  • A roll of 2 sends you back to 0. Don't roll a 2.
  • A roll of 7 is bad. It results in -7 points.
  • The first person to 20 points wins.
